**Last updated: February 23, 2026**

*This document is available in Portuguese for convenience. In case of conflict, the English version prevails.*

---

## Cancellation

### How to Cancel

You may cancel your subscription at any time by:

- Using the account settings within the Axiom Solver platform, or
- Sending an email to **support@axiom-solver.com**

### What Happens After Cancellation

- Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
- You will not be charged for subsequent billing cycles.
- After the billing period ends, your account will revert to the free tier (if available) or become inactive.
- Cancellation does not retroactively refund the current billing period.

---

## Refunds

### Eligibility

You may request a refund if:

- You request it within **7 days** of a charge, and
- You have not substantially used the paid features during that billing period.

### How to Request a Refund

Send an email to **support@axiom-solver.com** with your account email and the reason for your request. We will respond within 2 business days.

### Processing

- Approved refunds are issued to the **original payment method** used for the transaction.
- Refunds are typically processed within **5–10 business days**, depending on your bank or card issuer.
- Refunds are issued in **USD**, the currency of the original charge.

### Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will not be granted if:

- The request is made more than 7 days after the charge
- The paid features were substantially used during the billing period
- The account was suspended or terminated due to a violation of our Terms of Service
